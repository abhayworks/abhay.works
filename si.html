Satyagraha Institute

Training leaders in the traditions of nonviolence.


Client

Satyagraha Institute

Context

Satyagraha Institute is a nonprofit based in South Dakota, USA. They host a nonviolence training program once per year in 3 - 4 countries. Those who are interested in participating must complete an application and pay various fees.

The current application system for Satyagraha Institute is cumbersome for both applicants and the coordinators who review the applications. Applicants must navigate through a convoluted series of steps to submit their application while coordinators are required to autonomously keep all materials organized, potentially resulting in misplacement and general disarray.

College application software is too complex and job application software delivers within the wrong scope. Basic form applications like Google Forms and Typeform lack several crucial features.

Challenge

Since no existing application platforms fulfill the Institute’s needs, the challenge is to create an MVP of a comprehensive application system that allows:

1. Applicants to have full control and visibility over their complete application within a singular platform throughout the entirety of the process.

2. Coordinators to efficiently communicate with applicants and keep application materials organized.

Satyagraha Institute has the goal of becoming a “premiere” training center for nonviolence. Its internal infrastructure should be one of many facets that reflect such a vision.
